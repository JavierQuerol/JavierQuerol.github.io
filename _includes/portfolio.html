Remo

iOS and MacOSX app that connects your iOS device to your Mac and sees your video files, so you can use your iOS device as a remote controller for playing video files and watch it with subtitles without moving from the couch. It also supports Beamer and has iMDB information. Uses caching to minimize networking and pushes the UI to the limit

  • App Store: Link
  • Web: remo.re
  • Date: Feb 2014
  • Service: iOS & OSX

Subs

MacOSX app that downloads the subtitles for your videos automatically. The only thing you have to do is drag and drop your video file onto the menubar icon and the video will be played on your default video player with subtitles in your own language.

  • Open Source: GitHub
  • Web: javierquerol.es/subs
  • Date: October 2014
  • Service: OSX

One Two Tix

A small enterprise app to manage sold tickets for concerts and events. The app scans QR codes to validate the entry and uploads the data to a server periodically. The app was designed to be used in more than one device so manages diffs and merge of information. Uses CoreData and background queues with a lot of custom UI animations.

  • Client: onetwotix @ treeNovum
  • Date: January 2014
  • Service: iOS

Post Bus

This is the official PostBus Switzerland app that helps the user to know which is next bus or where is the closest stop. It also shows information related to tourist routes and many more. We also developed a framework in order to provide access to other teams to develop apps to be deployed inside the main app.

  • Client: PostBus Ltd @ treeNovum (ssm)
  • Date: March 2012
  • Service: iOS

PubliBike

Another PostBus app that shows all the bikes and spots available around Switzerland.

  • Client: PostBus Ltd @ treeNovum (ssm)
  • Date: July 2012
  • Service: iOS

SAP - Sales On Event

Enterprise app for the SAP salesmen that provides an easy way to manage their accounts, clients and meetings. We developed a modular framework that allows us to nest all the controllers in order to filter the information making the navigation easy and powerful.

  • Client: SAP @ treeNovum (ssm)
  • Date: May 2012
  • Service: iOS
